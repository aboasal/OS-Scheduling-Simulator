Test Case: Scenario B (Short-Job-Heavy / Efficiency Test)
Goal: Prove SJF yields a lower Average Waiting Time when multiple jobs are queued.

System Setup:
Time Quantum (RR): 3

Process Inputs:
ID   | Arrival | Burst
----------------------
P1   |   0     |  10
P2   |   0     |  2
P3   |   0     |  1
P4   |   0     |  2

Expected Observation:
Because they all arrive at Time 0, SJF will instantly sort them and execute the shortest jobs first (P3, then P2/P4, leaving P1 for last),
resulting in a massive reduction in Average Waiting Time compared to Round Robin.
