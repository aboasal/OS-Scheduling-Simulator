Test Case: Scenario A (Basic Mixed Workload)
Goal: Establish a baseline of how both algorithms handle a normal mix of processes.

System Setup:
Time Quantum (RR): 4

Process Inputs:
ID   | Arrival | Burst
----------------------
P1   |   0     |  5
P2   |   1     |  8
P3   |   3     |  2
P4   |   5     |  6

Expected Observation:
SJF will run P1 to completion. By time 5, P2, P3, and P4 will be waiting. SJF will cleverly pick P3 next (shortest burst).
Round Robin will simply cycle through them in order, pausing the longer jobs (P2 and P4) to give everyone a turn.
